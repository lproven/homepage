Hello, welcome to my homepage and thanks for visiting!
This is both a personal site for those who are curious
about me and an advertisement for me in my capacity as
a freelance journalist and IT consultant.
If you've bumped into me online somewhere, then you're
probably going to be more interested in the "personal"
and "links" pages of this site - these will tell you a
bit more about me as a person.
If you require computer advice, help and support, then
the "professional" section may be more informative. It
will tell you about the kind of work that I do and the
various types of computer that I support.
In brief - my services include designing, implementing
and supporting PC and Macintosh systems both in stand-
alone and network environments - business, charity and
home/personal.
This includes advice about the Internet, especially in
the areas of email and web access for small businesses
and charitable organizations. I do not do web design -
this site excepted! Today, there are many professional
individuals and companies specializing in this area. I
can, however, help you to find such services.
I also write about such matters for both technical and
non-technical audiences - my work has appeared in most
of the UK's major computer magazines.
I don't solely do computers: I also cover other things
including science fiction literary criticism.
This site also contains an archive of almost all of my
published work. Copyright restrictions mean that it is
not totally complete, and it may not be completely up-
to-date, but I'll post as much as I can.
In online communication you may notice a penchant that
I have for fully-justified text such as that which you
are reading now. If you look carefully you will notice
that it contains no "white space" - additional gaps in
between words to pad the lines out.
This effect requires a monospaced font such as Courier,
Lucida Console or Monaco. If your machine or browser is
not equipped with an appropriate font this may not work
correctly; you may be able to correct this by adjusting
your web browser's preferences appropriately.
Unless your browser window's very narrow this text will
appear even and straight down the right and left sides.
