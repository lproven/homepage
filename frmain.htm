This page contains a selection of my published writing. Clicking on the categories at the top displays a list of those pieces down the left hand side; click on one of the titles to read the piece. Newer pieces appear at the top.

My "Comment" column appears once a month in Network News magazine. This is a weekly title - other issues' columns are written by Jon Honeyball, Steve Harrington and Davey Winder.

Stand-alone reviews cover both hardware and software: some are head-to-head reviews covering two or more similar products.

Grouptest reviews are comparative pieces which form part of a group test or "Labs" article: an article comparing multiple similar products.

Features and box-outs are complete magazine features or parts thereof.

Finally come some book reviews, mainly of science fiction. These were first published in Vector, the critical journal of the BSFA, or on DiverseBooks.com or Amazon.com.
